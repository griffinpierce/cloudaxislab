Privacy Policy

At Cloud Axis Lab, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is secure. We collect personal information when you use our services, such as when you:

The personal information we may collect includes:

  • Name, email address, phone number

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

3. Data Protection and Security

We take your privacy seriously and implement strict security measures to protect your information. Our website is secured with encryption technology (SSL/TLS), and we follow industry best practices to prevent unauthorized access to your data. We also partner with trusted hosting providers who offer high-level security features, including firewalls, DDoS protection, and automated backups.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following cases:

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect usage data and improve your browsing experience. Cookies help us:
