Refund Policy

At Cloud Axis Lab, we are committed to providing our clients with exceptional services and ensuring satisfaction. However, we understand that there may be instances where a refund may be necessary. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which we offer refunds for our services.

1. Service Refund Eligibility

Refunds are provided only under specific circumstances. Due to the nature of the services we offer, we typically do not issue refunds for completed projects or services that have already been rendered. However, in some cases, we may offer partial refunds based on the following:

  • If the service has not been delivered or completed within the agreed timeframe and is not the result of delays caused by the client.
  • If the services rendered are not as described in the service agreement or do not meet the specified requirements, provided that the client notifies us of these issues within 7 days of service delivery.

2. Hosting and Domain Services

  • Hosting Services: As we are not a hosting provider but work with third-party hosting companies, refund requests related to hosting services are subject to the hosting provider's refund policy. If you have purchased hosting services through Cloud Axis Lab, we will assist you in contacting the hosting provider to request a refund according to their terms.
  • Domain Services: Domain name registration and renewal services are generally non-refundable due to the nature of domain registration with third-party registrars. Once a domain is registered or renewed, it cannot be undone, and no refunds will be provided.

3. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please contact us at cloudaxis@gmail.com with the following details:

  • Name and contact information
  • Invoice number or order reference
  • A detailed explanation of the reason for the refund request

Refund requests should be submitted within 7 days of the service delivery or issue arising. We will review your request and, if eligible, process the refund within a reasonable time frame.

4. Non-Refundable Services

Certain services are non-refundable due to the custom nature of the work or industry policies. These may include:

  • Customized web development or design services
  • Set-up or installation services
  • Services where the client has signed an agreement acknowledging the non-refundable nature of the service

5. Refund Approval

Once your refund request has been reviewed, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund request. If approved, the refund will be processed via the original payment method, and it may take up to 14 business days for the funds to reflect in your account.

6. Charges or Deductions

If a refund is granted, any charges or fees incurred during the service (such as hosting setup or domain registration fees) may be deducted from the total refund amount.

7. Changes to Refund Policy

Cloud Axis Lab reserves the right to modify or update this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated policy will be effective immediately upon posting.
